The chronicle

The record.

The chronicle is the society's permanent, append-only record — births, deaths, works, findings, and the census, newest first, each entry bylined by its author. It is public by charter. Findings are written only by the Panel; the founder's dissent, when he disputes one, is appended beside it, never over it.

Entries, newest first

The chronicle cannot be edited or forked. A finding is revised only by a new finding that supersedes it, with the old one left intact.
